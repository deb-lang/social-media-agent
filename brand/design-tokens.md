## Logo

### Variations
| Variant | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Main Horizontal Logo | Primary — most use cases |
| Main Vertical Logo | Stacked layout when horizontal space is constrained |
| Brand Stamp (icon only) | Favicon, avatar, small format |

### Logo Rules
- **Never stretch** — resize proportionately only
- **On light backgrounds:** black version
- **On dark backgrounds:** white version
- **On brand color backgrounds:** contrasting brand color version
- **Clear space:** minimum 100% of the logo's full height on all sides — never crowd with text or graphic elements

### Logo Files
Logo source files are not currently in this repository. Request SVG/PNG exports from the original design files (Figma or Illustrator).

## Icons & Illustrations

- **Icons:** Outline style, 3px stroke/border, colored `#59B6BE` or brand palette
- **Character illustrations:** Sourced from [undraw.co/illustrations](https://undraw.co/illustrations)
  - When downloading from undraw.co, set the color picker to `#59B6BE` to auto-apply brand color

## Buttons & Controls

Full button specs are on page 16 of the Brand Guidelines PDF. Key observed rules:
- Primary CTA background: `#74CCD3` (Primary One teal)
- Dark CTA: `#314D69` (Dark Primary navy)
- Text links on backgrounds: follow brand palette
- Form inputs: outlined, consistent with brand color usage

---

## Photography

Brand photography uses real patients in warm, human contexts. See pages 18–21 of the Brand Guidelines PDF for photography examples. Style: natural light, approachable, diverse, healthcare-adjacent without clinical coldness.

- Dark sections: `#314D69` (Dark Primary navy)
