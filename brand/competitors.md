## Competitive Categories

### Category 1: Pharma Hub / Patient Support (Operational Focus)

**Not direct competitors** — they solve access, enrollment, prior auth, copay. PatientPartner solves the "missing middle" (psychological barriers). Complementary, not competing.

| Company | What they do | Key messaging |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| **ConnectiveRx** | Hub services: enrollment, benefit verification, prior auth, fulfillment. 170+ pharma partners, 450+ brands, 90M patients | "Frictionless patient support." "Momentum at every patient access moment." |
| **Lash Group (Cencora)** | Patient support, hub services. 15M+ patients, 100+ programs, 20+ therapeutic areas | "Where innovation and heart drive patient access." "Operational excellence." |
| **McKesson Life Sciences** | Hub and patient support. Specialty pharma focus | "Transformation occurring in the industry." Segmenting by therapy type |

**Gap they don't fill:** Patients enroll, get prior auth, get copay—and still don't start. Fear, doubt, overwhelm. PatientPartner fills that gap with peer mentorship.

---

**White space:** No scaled, enterprise pharma-focused peer-to-peer mentor platform with real-time matching and compliance infrastructure. PatientPartner owns this.

## White Space (PatientPartner's Territory)

1. **Real-time peer-to-peer mentorship** — Connect when hesitation is highest, not weeks later. No coordinator-driven scheduling.
2. **The missing middle** — Solving psychological barriers (fear, doubt) that hubs and operational PSPs don't address
3. **Productized, scalable** — Deploy across brands and disease states without rebuilding. Less operational drag than Snow/Reverba.
4. **Pharma/med-tech native** — Built for commercial teams, not health systems. Integrates with pharma workflows
5. **Measurable ROI with specific numbers** — 68%, 71%, 90%, 29%, 133.5 days. Not vague "engagement"
6. **Trust shift** — "Patients prefer peer-to-peer over traditional methods." Data-backed positioning
